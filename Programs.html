Our Programs

At the University of Wollongong in Dubai (UOWD), we offer a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills needed for the modern workforce.

Undergraduate Programs

  • Bachelor of Business Administration

    Learn the fundamentals of business administration, management, and leadership.

  • Bachelor of Engineering (Honours)

    Specialize in various engineering disciplines including Mechanical, Electrical, and Civil Engineering.

  • Bachelor of Computer Science

    Gain expertise in programming, software development, and IT systems.

  • Bachelor of Communications and Media

    Explore the world of media, public relations, and communication strategies.

Postgraduate Programs

  • Master of Business Administration (MBA)

    Advance your career with an MBA that covers strategic management, finance, and marketing.

  • Master of Engineering Management

    Combine engineering expertise with management skills to lead projects and teams.

  • Master of Information Technology Management

    Develop advanced IT management skills to oversee technology projects and systems.

  • Master of International Studies

    Understand global politics, economics, and international relations.
