Admissions

At the University of Wollongong in Dubai (UOWD), we are committed to making the admissions process as straightforward as possible. Whether you are applying for an undergraduate or postgraduate program, we are here to guide you every step of the way.

Undergraduate Admissions

Eligibility Criteria

  • Completion of high school with a recognized secondary education certificate.
  • Minimum grades as specified for each program.
  • English language proficiency requirements.

Application Process

  • Submit an online application form through our website.
  • Upload required documents (transcripts, certificates, ID).
  • Pay the application fee.
  • Attend an interview (if required).
  • Receive your admission decision via email.

Postgraduate Admissions

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor's degree from a recognized university.
  • Minimum GPA as specified for each program.
  • English language proficiency requirements.
  • Relevant work experience (for certain programs).

Application Process

  • Submit an online application form through our website.
  • Upload required documents (transcripts, certificates, ID).
  • Pay the application fee.
  • Attend an interview (if required).
  • Receive your admission decision via email.

Important Dates

  • Application deadlines for Spring and Fall semesters.
  • Interview schedules (if applicable).
  • Orientation and start dates for new students.
