About Us

The University of Wollongong in Dubai (UOWD) is one of the UAE’s oldest and most prestigious universities. Established in 1993, UOWD is part of a global brand with a presence in Australia, Hong Kong, Malaysia, and more.

UOWD offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Business, Engineering, Information Sciences, Humanities, and Social Sciences. Our diverse student body, representing over 108 nationalities, creates a dynamic and inclusive learning environment.

Our mission is to provide world-class education and foster research and innovation that addresses the challenges of the 21st century. We are committed to nurturing graduates who are equipped with the skills and knowledge to excel in the global workforce.

Our Vision

To be a leading international university that supports the UAE’s vision for higher education and contributes to the development of the region through quality education and research.

Our Values

  • Excellence: Striving for excellence in teaching, research, and community engagement.
  • Integrity: Upholding the highest ethical standards in all our endeavors.
  • Diversity: Embracing diversity and promoting inclusivity.
  • Innovation: Encouraging innovative thinking and solutions.

Our Campus

Located in the heart of Dubai Knowledge Park, our state-of-the-art campus provides a stimulating environment for learning and research. With modern facilities, cutting-edge technology, and a vibrant campus life, UOWD is the ideal place to pursue your academic and professional goals.
